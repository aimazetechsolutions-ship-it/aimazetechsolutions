FORM EMAIL SETUP

The contact form is now configured to send inquiries to:
info@aimazetechsolutions.com

How it works:
1. The contact form posts to FormSubmit using this action:
   https://formsubmit.co/info@aimazetechsolutions.com
2. The first time a form is submitted, FormSubmit may send a confirmation email to info@aimazetechsolutions.com. Open that email and confirm activation.
3. After confirmation, future form submissions will be emailed to info@aimazetechsolutions.com.

If you host on Netlify:
- The form also includes data-netlify=true and form-name.
- In Netlify dashboard, go to Site configuration > Forms > Form notifications and add info@aimazetechsolutions.com as email notification recipient.
